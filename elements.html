My Research Project

What affects Housing Price? Evidence from California in 1990

I analyze the factors that can explain the California housing market in 1990. I assume that the median income has critical effects on housing prices. By employing OLS regression, I find a positive linear relationship between median income and median house value. The OLS results suggest another covariate that also plays a significant role in affecting housing prices. Ocean proximity is a critical factor that indicates the house location's effects on the housing price. The visualization outcome also shows that housing price reflects the environmental characteristics at different places. Based on my finding, income and the house's location are two critical factors that explain California's house values.
